# Finance & Operations KPI Dashboard (FY 2023)

![Dashboard Preview](screenshots/dashboard_full.png)

## Project Overview
This project showcases an **executive-level Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze **Finance and Operations KPIs** for FY 2023.  
The dashboard supports leadership decision-making by providing visibility into revenue performance, customer mix, regional trends, and cost leakage drivers.

---

## Business Objectives
- Track **Total Revenue (YTD)** and customer-level monetization
- Identify **cost leakage** and operational inefficiencies
- Analyze **revenue trends** over time
- Compare **regional performance**
- Enable **root cause analysis** for cost optimization

---

## Key KPIs
- **Total Revenue (USD) – YTD**
- **Cost Leakage (USD) – Variance**
- **Average Revenue per Customer**

![KPI Section](screenshots/kpi_section.png)

---

## Dashboard Components

### 1️. Monthly Revenue Trend
- Shows month-over-month revenue movement
- Helps identify growth patterns and seasonal behavior

### 2️. Revenue Contribution by Customer Segment
- Enterprise vs SMB revenue mix
- Enables strategic customer segmentation insights

### 3️. Revenue by Region
- Compares regional revenue contribution (APAC, EMEA)
- Supports geographic performance analysis

### 4️. Top Products by Cost Leakage
- Highlights products contributing most to cost overruns
- Supports **root cause analysis (RCA)** and cost optimization initiatives

![Cost Leakage Analysis](screenshots/cost_leakage_analysis.png)

---

## Key Insights
- A small subset of products contributes disproportionately to cost leakage
- Revenue is diversified across customer segments with balanced contribution
- Regional revenue performance remains stable across major markets
